Games

New games I played in 2017


The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild


There was a lot of hype around this release and it really did deliver. BOTW is really really good and was the game this year that I sank hours and hours into. The mechanics of the game work so well with all the puzzles and the environment is stunning. The game offers almost no help on how to play and leaves you to figure everything out - literally. If you think it would work; it probably does. It's these simple things that make it such a well-rounded and fun game.


Will definitely be playing this for a long time.


Fire Emblem Echoes: Shadows of Valentia


My personal favourite game of 2017. I've always been a Fire Emblem fan but Echoes' gorgeous artwork and soundtrack is what really made me love this remake of a 1992 game. The difficulty is a bit on the tough side but very fulfilling. Although the maps can be a bit tedious, the gameplay, art style and music all overshadow this making each battle feel pretty damn epic. I loved the art so much I bought the official artbook and soundtrack during my trip in Japan.


Absolutely love it.


PLAYERUNKNOWN'S BATTLEGROUNDS


ESPORTS READY. Probably the most worst game I've played in recent memory and for sure the most poorly optimized game I have ever played. Seriously spend your $40 on literally anything else or play Fortnite which is free.


$40 I'm never getting back.
