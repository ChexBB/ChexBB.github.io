Japan 日本

April 四月


Tokyo 東京

It had always been my childhood dream to go to Japan. My parents would always tease or hint at bringing me one day. Even after all these years, I've still wanted to go there so much. When the plane pulled just into sight of the Tokyo coastline I looked out of the window and at the sunrise. I fan-girled a little. Seeing the rising sun of the Japanese flag was so much more breath taking than I had ever imagined. Photos do not do this view justice. (And definately not the one I took below haha)
